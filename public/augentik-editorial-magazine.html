Origin Story

Origin Story

Built because finance work breaks generic AI.

2024

founding year listed on the live Augentik site

The thesis is simple: general-purpose tools are fine right up until the workflow becomes regulated. Finance teams need answers that survive review, not polished guesses. So Augentik starts with the rules, stays in domain, and makes the citation part of the product, not an afterthought.

  • Start from the actual guidance, not a broad model memory.
  • Design for memo prep, audit evidence, and position support from the start.
  • Treat compliance as a living operating layer, not a quarterly scramble.
  • Precision is the product in regulated finance workflows.
  • Purpose-built for GAAP, SEC, and SOX, not general Q&A.
  • Every answer should be safe enough to move into documentation.
